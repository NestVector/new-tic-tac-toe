🎲 How to Play

🎯 Goal: Be the first player to reach square 100!
🎲 Rolling: Press Roll! to roll the dice and move your token that many squares forward.
🪜 Ladders (yellow lines): Land on the bottom of a ladder and zoom up to a higher square — lucky you!
🐸 Hazards (coloured animals): Land on a hazard head and slide down to a lower square — watch out!
🏆 Winning: First player to land exactly on square 100 wins! (You must roll the exact number or stay put.)
🤖 AI Players: Toggle the switch on a player slot to let the computer play that turn automatically.

❌ How to Play

🎯 Goal: Get 3 in a row — across, down, or diagonally!
❌ X goes first. Players take turns clicking empty squares to place their mark.
🏆 Winning: The first player to fill a full row, column, or diagonal wins!
🤝 Draw: If all 9 squares are filled and nobody has 3 in a row, it's a draw!
🤖 AI Mode: Click Switch to AI Mode to play against the computer. Change difficulty in Settings.
↩️ Undo: Made a mistake? Hit Undo (human vs human only) to take back your last move.
